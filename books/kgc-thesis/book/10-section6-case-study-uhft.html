6.8 Lessons Learned

Technical Insights

  1. L1 Cache is Critical: 99.9% of execution time must stay in L1 cache to hit <10Œºs
  2. Branchless Code: Conditional jumps kill performance; field geometry eliminates branches
  3. FPGA Preprocessing: Offload parsing and canonicalization to FPGA saves 50-100Œºs
  4. Async Persistence: Never block critical path on disk I/O

Architectural Patterns
