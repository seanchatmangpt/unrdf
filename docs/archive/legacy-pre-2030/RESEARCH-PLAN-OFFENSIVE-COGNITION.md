# Research Plan: Offensive Compiled Cognition (The David Strategy)

## 1. Objective
Establish a superior architectural terrain that renders incumbent runtime AI platforms obsolete for bounded enterprise work. This plan moves beyond defensive validation into an offensive capture of the "decision river" through the construction of the **Blue River Dam**.

## 2. Theoretical Invariants (The Sling)
Research must focus on three load-bearing invariants that incumbents cannot adopt without structural self-cannibalization:
- **Residency ($C_{compiled} \in Artifact$)**: Cognition is a property of the binary, not a service.
- **Latency Collapse ($R \ll 1$)**: Symbolic reasoning as execution physics, not advisory overhead.
- **Deterministic Replay ($f_{compiled}(x_t, v) = y_t$)**: Audit by artifact inspection rather than log interpretation.

## 3. Offensive Research Streams

### Stream 1: Runtime Demand Displacement (RDD)
- **Question**: How much incumbent inference volume can be captured upstream?
- **Metric**: RDD = (Runtime Decisions Eliminated) / (Baseline Decisions).
- **Target**: Prove RDD > latest for stable enterprise use cases (Insurance, E-commerce, Manufacturing).

### Stream 2: Cognitive Breeding & Manufacturability
- **Question**: Can specialized cognitive breeds be manufactured faster than generic platforms can generalize?
- **Action**: Use Sean's throughput (9k commits/30B tokens) as the baseline for a new production function: $C_{breed} = Closure + Generation + Certification$.

### Stream 3: Asymmetric Force Application (The Dachshund Doctrine)
- **Question**: Can compact, fitted cognition displace oversized generalized models in "burrowed" terrain?
- **Action**: Benchmark "Minimum Decisive Force" (MDF) primitives in IoT and Drone environments where connectivity, power, and latency are adversarial to cloud AI.

## 4. Competitive Counter-Intelligence
- **Red Teaming**: Anticipate incumbent-funded research that tries to collapse CC into "TinyML" or "Model Compilation."
- **Offensive Gate**: Introduce the **Runtime Justification Requirement**. Any paper proposing a runtime model for a bounded decision must prove why it *cannot* be compiled.

## 5. Execution Timeline
- **Phase 1 (The Shepherd)**: Stabilize local $O^*$ in `unrdf` and `kgc-4d`.
- **Phase 2 (The Giant-Slayer)**: Release the Compiled Cognition flagship paper and RDD benchmarks.
- **Phase 3 (The King)**: Launch the nDim Marketplace as the distribution layer for certified cognitive lineages.
