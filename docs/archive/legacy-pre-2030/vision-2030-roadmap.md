It is no longer a "consultant" to be asked; it is the "physics" of the system that prevents unlawful state transitions in real-time. ### 2.3 The Blue River Dam (Market Redirection)
The current AI market is a "Red Ocean" of competing LLM providers. Unrdf builds a **Blue River Dam** upstream. By the time a decision intent reaches a cloud provider, the Unrdf ecosystem has already captured, compiled, and executed it locally. This redirects the profit pools of the AI era toward the manufacturers of cognitive artifacts rather than the providers of raw compute.
