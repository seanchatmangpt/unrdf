# The Dachshund Doctrine: Asymmetric Force in Bounded Terrain

## 1. Overview
The **Dachshund Doctrine** is a theory of cognitive morphology for autonomous systems (Drones, IoT, Probes) operating in constrained, adversarial, or GPS-denied environments. It shifts the design goal from "General Intelligence" (The Talking Dog) to "Fitted Force" (The Tunnel Dog).

## 2. Evolved Cognition: The Dachshund vs. The Badger
In the "Red Ocean" of AI, incumbents act as badgers—entrenched, fierce, and reliant on mass. Most AI startups attempt to become bigger badgers. The Dachshund Doctrine rejects this, favoring a system designed for the **Burrow**:
- **Low Profile**: Compact artifacts that fit inside edge hardware where platforms cannot.
- **Trace Persistence**: Following "scent-lines" (weak signals/sensor data) through constrained paths.
- **Adversarial Confrontation**: The courage to decide locally under comms loss.
- **Return Path Memory**: Bounded recall gates to ensure mission safety.

## 3. Cognitive Functions of the Drone Breed
A "Dachshund-Class" drone does not need to write poetry; it needs to solve for **Lawful Motion in Confined Space**:

### A. Scent-Line Following (Trace Signal)
$$PathHypothesis_t = Follow(TraceSignal_{t-1})$$
Using compiled symbolic lattices (MYCIN-style) to follow acoustic, thermal, or network anomalies.

### B. The Vertical Burrow (Structural Inference)
Just as the urban dachshund retargets burrow instinct to tree canopies, the drone retargets its pursuit logic to new terrains (e.g., sewers, ducts, network subnets) without breaking its core mission invariants.

### C. Minimum Decisive Force (MDF)
We do not use an LLM loop to count sheep. We use a compiled primitive to apply the smallest precise pressure needed to restore order.
- **Example**: A brief, calibrated network-throttle instead of a total subnet shutdown.

## 4. Operational Invariants for IoT/Drones
1. **Mission Bounded**: Autonomy is local, but purpose is shepherd-aligned.
2. **Deterministic Execution**: Identical sensor inputs MUST produce identical kinetic outputs.
3. **Artifact Resident**: No cloud consultation required for the "tunnel" mission.
4. **Admissible Release**: Action ($A$) is gated by current $O^*$ (battery, signal, law).

## 5. Summary
The Dachshund Doctrine proves that **Apparent Size $\neq$ Effective Force**. By manufacturing cognitive breeds fitted to terrain, we displace generalized AI platforms. We are not "smaller" AI; we are **Compact Force**—bred for the burrow that incumbents cannot defend.
