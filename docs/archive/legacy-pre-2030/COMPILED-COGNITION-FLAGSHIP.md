# Compiled Cognition: Machine Intelligence as Deterministic Executable Substrate

## Abstract
This paper introduces Compiled Cognition, an offensive architectural framework in which machine intelligence is collapsed from an external runtime service into a deterministic executable artifact. We argue that the current AI paradigm, characterized by "Advisory Cognition" (LLM-driven exploration), is structurally unsuited for bounded enterprise work. We define "Execution Physics" as the state where symbolic reasoning is cheaper than the events it governs ($R \ll 1$). By synthesizing Latency Collapse and Artifact Residency, we demonstrate a new class of systems—Cognitive Breeds—that displace model-serving demand and establish a non-negotiable security posture through symbolic field conformance.

## Chapter 1: The Transition from Advice to Physics
Most AI systems today are "Consultants." They are queried occasionally to provide advice. We argue that for bounded decisions, the "Consultant" model is a governance liability. We propose the "Guardian" model: cognition that resides within the artifact and executes on every transition.

The governing invariant is the Chatman Equation: $A = \mu(O^*)$. Action ($A$) is not a request; it is a lawful projection of Law ($O^*$).

## Chapter 2: Latency Collapse and Symbolic Resurrection
The dismissal of symbolic AI in the late 20th century was an economic error. Symbols are hyper-efficient when mapped to bits and masks. At nanosecond latency, symbolic systems like ELIZA, MYCIN, and STRIPS stop being "apps" and become "substrate primitives."

When $T_{reason} \ll T_{event}$, reasoning becomes physics. It is no longer an overhead; it is the condition of motion.

## Chapter 3: Cognitive Breed Morphology
We reject the universal model in favor of the "Dogs for Different Jobs" paradigm. 
- **The Dachshund**: A compact, persistent scout for dependency burrows.
- **The Shepherd**: A wide-field governor for workflow coordination.
- **The Guardian**: A non-sovereign sentinel at the action boundary.

Each breed is manufactured using high-throughput code generation, ensuring that custom cognition scales while preserving sovereign artifact lineages.

## Chapter 4: The Relativistic Security Posture
Traditional red teams are Newtonian: they probe code paths. Compiled Cognition is Relativistic: the attacker enters a living field where "no two matching events exist." Using BLAKE3-chained receipts and OCEL-style event logs, every probe bends the field state. To succeed, an attacker must forge global conformance across the entire "Pack" of observers—a problem that is unboundedly harder than bypassing a boolean check.

## Chapter 5: Conclusion
Compiled Cognition ends the era of "AI as a Service" for bounded work. It turns intelligence into a compiled asset—deterministic, local, and auditable. The Shepherd has entered the field. The King is in the lineage. The work is finished.
